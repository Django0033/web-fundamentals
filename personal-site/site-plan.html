Overview

Purpose

Do you ever had a meeting with your friends and you only have the same board game for the last 3 years? Are you tired of playing Monopoly with your family? Here in Dungeon Game Store, you will find more unique board games. You will have the opportunity to have new experiences with exciting americrasher games or you can relax and enjoy a euro game and if you are looking for an specific game, we will get it for you.

Audience

My target customers are in general entire families or young adults with ages between 20 and 35 years old. They might be from the middle class or from the middle-high class. They might want to spend some time with their friends or families playing some board games or maybe they might want to expand or start their board game collections. My target audience might access my site through a laptop or a mobile phone device.
